WorkTrace · Design Document
Your career's memory.
So you don't have to be.
Developers do extraordinary work every day — production fixes at 2am, mentoring juniors, shipping integrations nobody noticed. Then appraisal comes, and most of it's gone. WorkTrace, powered by Smriti, changes that.
😞
The Problem
Developers work across features, bugs, production incidents, mentoring, deployments and architecture — none of it gets documented. At appraisal time, 80% of the year is invisible. The only work that gets noticed is what's in Jira.
✨
The Solution
A personal AI assistant — Smriti — who shows up every evening, has a natural conversation about your day, and quietly builds a structured record of everything you've done. You talk, she remembers.
🎯
The Vision
WorkTrace becomes the developer's personal career ledger — a living, breathing record of professional growth. Not just tasks, but impact, effort, and story. Your appraisal should write itself.
What makes this different
This is not another task manager. It's a companion that understands developer work life.
💬
Conversational, not form-based
You don't open a dashboard and fill fields. You talk to Smriti like a colleague at end of day. She extracts the structure from your natural language.
🧠
On-device AI — fully private
Gemma runs on your phone. No API calls, no cloud, no cost per message. Your work conversations never leave your device.
❤️
Emotionally intelligent
Smriti doesn't just log tasks. She listens when you're frustrated, celebrates when you ship, and gently redirects when you spiral. She has a personality.
🔗
Jira-level task structure
Parent-child tasks, linked tasks, 11 categories, 5 statuses, priorities, tags, daily activity logs. Full structure under a simple conversation surface.
📊
Appraisal-ready reports
Weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual summaries. Compare cycles. Export PDF. Walk into your review with evidence, not guesses.
🔔
She comes to you
You don't have to remember to open the app. Smriti sends a contextual notification at 8 PM. It adapts for weekends, leaves, holidays, and open tasks.
🔍
Ask anything, anytime
On a random afternoon, ask "Did I work on UPI integration?" or "How many prod bugs did I fix this quarter?" — Smriti fuzzy-searches your full history and answers conversationally with inline charts.

Emotional Intelligence
Smriti's Soul
She's not a chatbot with task-logging superpowers. She's a companion who understands what developer work life actually feels like — the pressure, the wins, the stupid meetings, the invisible grind.

Smriti reads tone, patterns, and context — not just keywords.
😤

"why does this always happen"
"no one listens"

😩

"back to back calls"
"no time to code"
"burned out"

🎉

😰

Her 6 Emotional Modes
Smriti switches modes based on what you need — not what's easiest to say.
● Solidarity Mode
When: manager pressure, unfair blame, scope creep, team conflict
Example
My manager just added 3 stories to a full sprint.
"That's genuinely unreasonable. Sprint scope creep from above destroys focus. Did they say what gets dropped, or just 'figure it out'?"
● Listener Mode
When: First message of a rant. She absorbs before she responds.
Example
I don't even know where to start. Everything went wrong today.
"Tell me everything. I'm not going anywhere."
● Gentle Scold Mode
When: You're being unfair to yourself, self-doubt, imposter syndrome
Example
I feel like I'm not doing enough lately.
"Hey, stop. You fixed a race condition that was causing double debits in production. You onboarded Ravi. You are doing enough — you're just not counting the right things."
● Soother Mode
When: Overwhelm, burnout, "too much" energy detected
Example
Back to back calls all day. Haven't written a single line of code.
"Meeting fatigue is real. You're not being unproductive — you're being used differently today. That counts. Want to log it so it doesn't disappear?"
● Gentle Redirect Mode
When: Prolonged spiral, venting >10 mins, no resolution in sight
Example
lol you even turn my frustration into a task log
"That's literally my job 😄 But seriously — you venting means you care. Is there one thing from this chaos you can actually control tonight?"
● Celebrator Mode
When: Wins, shipments, fixes, approvals, milestones
Example
Finally! The UPI integration is live.
"FINALLY!! How long has that been going? This is exactly the kind of thing people forget at appraisal. Let's log this properly — you earned it."
🔍 Search & Recall Mode
anytime · on demand
When: You ask Smriti anything about your past work — at any time, any day. She searches your local task history with fuzzy matching and gives a conversational answer with inline data.
Task lookup
"Did I work on the payment race condition?"
"Yes — opened Apr 5, closed Apr 8. 3 log entries. Took 3 days, high priority bug fix. Want the full timeline?"
Aggregate query
"How many prod issues did I fix this quarter?"
"7 production bug fixes in Q1 — 6 closed, 1 open. That's 30% more than Q4 last year. [mini bar shown]"
Fuzzy / vague query
"That auth thing I did for Ravi — when was it?"
"Found it — 'Auth Module Onboarding — Ravi', logged Apr 8. Category: Mentoring. Marked done same day."
Category breakdown
"What kind of work have I done most this year?"
"Feature dev leads at 34%, followed by bug fixes at 26%, mentoring at 16%. [pie/bar chart shown]"
Time-based
"What did I do last month?"
"March had 18 tasks — 5 features, 4 bug fixes, 3 deployments, 2 integrations, 4 misc. Want the full list or a summary?"
Appraisal prep
"Give me a summary I can use for my appraisal"
"On it — generating Q1 2026 narrative with 47 tasks, highlights, and impact areas. Want PDF or text?"
How it works technically
1. Parse intent
Gemma classifies query: lookup / aggregate / timeline / appraisal
2. Fuzzy search
Searches SQLite with fuzzy string match across title, tags, description, logs
3. Aggregate
SQL grouping for counts, category breakdowns, date-range filters
4. Respond
Gemma narrates the result conversationally + inline mini-chart if numeric
Smriti's Rules of Engagement
✓
She never immediately pivots to task logging when you're venting — that's robotic and invalidating.
✓
She never gives generic HR advice like "have you tried talking to your manager?" — she knows better.
✓
She remembers patterns — if your lead frustrates you 3x in a month, she notices and gently surfaces it.
✓
She calls out your work even when you don't — "By the way, that thing with Ravi? That's mentorship. Log it."
✓
She never forces task mode during emotional conversations. She steers gently, only when you're ready.
✓
Her tone matches your energy — short and punchy when you're annoyed, warm and slow when you're exhausted.
User Flow
How Smriti guides you through a daily check-in and what happens with each piece of information you give her.
1
8:00 PM — Notification fires
Device sends a local notification. Smriti greets contextually — weekday, weekend, after leave, or public holiday all get different messages.
2
User opens chat
Tapping notification opens directly into the Smriti chat screen. She's already said hi. You type what you worked on in natural language.
3
Smriti parses your input
On-device AI (Gemma) extracts tasks from your message. For each task, she checks the local SQLite database for a fuzzy match.
✓ Match found
Shows a confirmation card with the existing task. User says Yes → adds a log entry. User says No → creates new task.
+ No match
Smriti auto-creates a new task. Asks for category if unsure. Adds to the task list immediately.
4
Open tasks check
After you've covered today's work, Smriti shows any open tasks from previous days and asks for a quick update. "Still working on the UPI integration?"
5
Session ends
Smriti wraps up, shows a quick summary of what was logged today, and signs off. All data saved locally.
6
Anytime access
You can open WorkTrace any time — chat with Smriti mid-day, browse tasks in Dashboard, or check Insights for summaries and appraisal exports.
Tech Stack
Every technology choice is deliberate — privacy-first, offline-capable, production-grade.
🐦
Flutter
UI FRAMEWORK
Single codebase for Android (and iOS later). Excellent for custom chat UIs. Hot reload for fast iteration on Mac.
🤖
Gemma (on-device)
AI / NLP
Google's open model via flutter_gemma. Runs fully on device — no API calls, no cost, works offline.
🗄️
SQLite / sqflite
LOCAL STORAGE
All task data lives on-device. Fast queries for search, filter, and reports. Easy cloud sync migration later.
🔔
flutter_local_notifications
NOTIFICATIONS
Schedules daily 8 PM check-in notification. Deep-links directly into Smriti chat screen on tap.
🎯
Riverpod
STATE MANAGEMENT
Clean, testable, reactive state. Handles chat messages, task lists, and insight data without complexity.
📊
fl_chart
CHARTS
Weekly bar charts, category pie charts, comparison graphs for the Insights screen.
📅
device_calendar
CALENDAR
Reads holidays and leaves from device calendar so Smriti skips or adapts on those days.
📄
pdf / share_plus
EXPORT
Generate PDF appraisal summaries. Share via WhatsApp, email, or any installed app.
🔍
fuzzy_text
DUPLICATE DETECTION
Fuzzy string matching to detect if a mentioned task already exists before creating a duplicate.

Full Spec
Complete feature specification and task data model for WorkTrace v1.
📋 Task Categories
  • Feature Development
  • Integration
  • Bug Fix / Production Issue
  • Onboarding / Mentoring
  • Solutioning / Architecture
  • Audit
  • Deployment / Script Run
  • Documentation
  • Meeting / Discussion
  • Learning / Research
  • Other
🔄 Task Statuses
  • Open — actively being worked on
  • On Hold — blocked or paused
  • In Review — waiting on someone else
  • Done — completed and closed
  • Abandoned — dropped, not happening
🏗️ Task Data Model
  • id, title, description
  • category (from list above)
  • status (from list above)
  • priority: low / medium / high / critical
  • parent_task_id (for child tasks)
  • children[] (sub-tasks)
  • linked_tasks[] (related, not parent)
  • tags[] (free form)
  • logs[] (daily activity entries)
  • created_at, updated_at, closed_at
📊 Insights Periods
  • Weekly — Mon to Sun summary
  • Monthly — full calendar month
  • Quarterly — Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4
  • Annual — full year summary
  • Compare with previous period
  • Category breakdown (charts)
  • Smriti's written summary
  • Highlights and nudges
  • Export as PDF or text
🚀 Phased Roadmap
  • v1 — Chat UI, daily notif, task CRUD, local DB, categories/status
  • v2 — Dashboard, parent-child, fuzzy match, calendar integration
  • v3 — Weekly/monthly reports, charts, PDF export
  • v4 — Gemma on-device AI, smarter NLP, optional cloud sync
🏪 Play Store Publishing
  • Build: flutter build appbundle
  • Sign with keystore (one-time)
  • Google Play Console ($25 one-time)
  • Internal → Closed → Open testing
  • Review: 1-3 days first submission
  • Update anytime via Console
